Enroll in SAT and ACT Workshops



Students and parents of all grades are welcome! Come, bring your friends, and learn about the college admissions process and how you (or your child) can best manage it! Please register below so we can account for all attendees.

PTPS is proud to offer different workshops for high school students for Fall 2012. Our workshops are meticulously planned and executed by our instructors, and are critical for college preparation. Registration for each workshop is available online; the workshops are open to any interested students, and are completely free of charge. Please register in advance in order to guarantee a spot. Please attend workshops with note-taking materials and questions, in order to make the best of your time.

Unless otherwise noted, all of our workshops will be held at YMCA-PG and E Teen Center. located at 2111 Martin Luther King Jr. Way, Berkeley, CA 94704. Click here for a map.

Please email workshop coordinator Hanui Choi at ptps.berkeley.workshop@gmail.com with any questions or concerns. We look forward to seeing you!
