Enroll in a Course



All PTPS courses are open to all high school students. All classes are free.

Students are free to attend any of our sites. Each course is 10 weeks long. Weekday classes meet twice a week for 2 hours each session. Weekend classes meet once a week for a total of 4 1/2 hours: Math for 2 hours, then a 30 minute break, followed by Verbal for 2 hours. Each SAT class is the same, so attending multiple sites is not beneficial. We ask that you commit to attending at least 80% of the classroom sessions, so please enroll only if you are serious about taking the course.

To register, please fill out the form below for your desired site. Once you have registered, you are enrolled in the course! You are welcome to attend a class, even if the first few meetings have already occurred. We prefer, however, that you enroll online before beginning to attend! There is no deadline for this enrollment.
